Mission Statment

Not all repairs to a vehicle need to be done at a repair shop. There are easy to do jobs that can be done from the comfort of your driveway or garage. Certain repair or fixees are not very complicated enabling owners to save money and time. Our goal is to guide you through the process with easy to follow directions. Please leave your thoughts and concerns below.

Repair DIY Mechanic Reason
Water Pump The water pump in the majority of vehicles is not acessiable without fully lifting the vehicle. This repair is often complex requiring specialty tools to remove and replace the pump.
Battery Replacement A 12 Volt battery is essential for a vehicle to run. A battery can be tested and replaced at a local auto parts store, but it will cost significantly more than purchasing a battery at a local Walmart. The replacement is simple, requiring only a standard socket or wrench set.
Suspension Replacement Repair or total replacement of a vehicle requires that the body be lifted and supported until the replacement or repair is complete. Specialty tools will be necessary depending on the brand of the car. This job can be dangerous for professionals since suspension parts are constantly compressed..
Engine Air Filter Engine Air Filters are safe and easy to replace without specialty tools. The air filter lives under the hood of your cars and usually requires clips to be unsnapped or a couple of Philips / flathead screws to be removed.
